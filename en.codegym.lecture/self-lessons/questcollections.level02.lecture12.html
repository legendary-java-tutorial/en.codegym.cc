RMI in practice

We'll consider a rather interesting topic: RMI. This stands for Remote Method Invocation. You can use RMI to allow two programs to communicate with each other, even if they are on different computers. Does that sound cool? :) And it's not so difficult to do! In this lesson, we'll analyze the elements of the RMI interaction and figure out how to configure it.

And finally, some good examples of using StringWriter.
